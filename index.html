ALONE WITH DEATH

Play action mode Play survival mode

Description

Alone with Death is a post apocalyptic zombie top-down view shooter.

ACTION MODE

In action mode your purpose is to survive from 5 waves of zombies. With each wave zombies become stronger and faster than other one. At start Player has only a pistol and some ammo. From time to time there will be generated more different guns and ammo.

SURVIVAL MODE

(In development) Also called "relax mode", where you are given an open world with full free of action. Player has 4 stats – health, hunger, endurance and cold. The Purpose – take a pleasure in survival process. You can explore the ruined world, kill zombies, eat, bask in the fire.

User guide

Weapon

There are 4 types of gun. At start you have a piston. With time new gun will be generated on a map.

  • pistol

  • AK-47

  • M4A1

  • AWP

Items

Occasionally ammo for weapon, aid kits and flak jacket will be generated on a map. Don't miss them. To activate aid kit just switch to its inventory cell.

  • pistol

  • AK-47

  • M4A1

  • AWP

  • standart aid kit

  • extended aid kit

Controls

  • Use these ones to switch between inventory cells

  • Use left mouse click or space to make shot

  • Use these ones to move a player

  • R for reload a gun

  • Use T to open your backpack

  • M to open a minimap

  • Use shift to run away from zombies
